## 4. Discussion notes

**Q1.** Why is the temperature still increasing even though the UK is using cleaner energy, and should the UK return to older energy sources?  
**A:** Global cumulative emissions are still increasing. Emissions depend on both energy demand and the carbon intensity of energy, and many countries still rely heavily on fossil fuels. Climate inertia also means that CO₂ stays in the atmosphere for a long time and oceans absorb much of the excess heat, which can delay temperature changes. Therefore, returning to older energy sources would not solve the problem.
